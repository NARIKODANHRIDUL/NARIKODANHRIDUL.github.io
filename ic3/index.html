What is IC3 ?
The International Cybersecurity Championship and Conference (IC3) event is designed to attract top talent and raise global awareness of the power of games to upskill the cybersecurity industry. Through its exciting, competition-based forum, the IC3 helps individuals further their cybersecurity education and skills

The Annual International Career & College Counseling (IC3) Conference & Expo is a hybrid gathering, held each August to bring together high schools and higher education representatives for a collaborative dialogue on issues that prompt solutions in career and college counseling practices.The conference features breakout sessions and networking opportunities for school leaders, counselors, teachers, university leaders, university admission representatives, and educational organizations to learn, connect, and build relationships.
