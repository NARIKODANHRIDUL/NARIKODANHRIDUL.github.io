Game On
   In October 2022, I had the privilege of leading a team of five members in organising a remarkable sports event for our first-year students – "GAME ON." This sports event was a huge success, with over 120 enthusiastic students eagerly participating. In a world where many people are more interested in social media than their health, "GAME ON" encouraged our students to pay attention to their physical and mental well-being.

   The sports lineup featured Badminton, Table Tennis, and Chess, providing a platform for our juniors to unearth their hidden talents. This event not only gave our budding athletes to showcase their skills but also helped our Physical Education department in identifying future talents for specialized training programs. Our objectives were crystal clear: to instill a sense of sportsmanship among our participants and to nurture athletes of exceptional caliber who would proudly represent our college on various stages. The "GAME ON" also offered them an invaluable opportunity to make connections with their peers, fostering a sense of camaraderie and belonging.

My contributions
  • Led a team of 5 members to successfully initiate and organize
  • Managing Google forms and organizing the details of the participants in excel
  • Video editing for the presentation of the event
