CompatrIoT
The CompatrlOT board is a versatile tool that is perfect for both embedded development and IoT security training. With its all-in-one design and industry-standard microcontrollers, CompatrloT offers a comprehensive learning experience that covers all the basics of communication protocols, ports, and IoT security. Whether you're looking to master UART, 12C, SPI, JTAG, WiFi or BLE, CompatrloT has you covered. In addition, its gamified labs and real-world vulnerability challenges will help you develop a deep understanding of how to sniff and manipulate communication for successful exploitation of IoT devices. Whether you're just starting out or seeking to enhance your skills, the multipurpose CompatrloT board is the ideal choice for anyone looking to advance their IoT security knowledge.

This is a multi purpose board, which can be used for development or for conducting security trainings. Our all-in-one board is equipped with industry-standard microcontrollers and offers a comprehensive learning experience covering all the basics of OT, IoT and Automotive security.
The CompatrIoT board is designed and developed by the team bi0s hardware collaborating with a startup company Traboda CyberLabs.

Whether you're just starting or looking to enhance your skills, CompatrIoT has everything you need to advance your IoT security knowledge. Our board is perfect for:
  • Embedded developers looking to hone their skills.
  • Cyber-security professionals seeking hands-on OT, IoT and Automotive security training.
  • Students and researchers exploring the world of Hardware.*
